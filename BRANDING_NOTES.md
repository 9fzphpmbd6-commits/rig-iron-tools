# Branding Notes – Texas Power Tool Storefront

## Candidate Brand Names

1. **Iron Star Drill Supply** – Combines the Texas "lone star" with industrial iron/steel. Strong, memorable.
2. **Rig Iron Tools** – Evokes Texas oil rigs and heavy steel work. Short and punchy.
3. **Panhandle Holemakers** – Geographic Texas reference + direct product description.
4. **Hill Country Mag & Ream** – Texas region + specific product types. Feels niche and expert.
5. **Lone Star Steel Supply** – Classic Texas branding, broad enough for power tools.

## Final Choice

**Rig Iron Tools**

### Rationale

- Two words, easy to say, easy to remember
- "Rig" evokes Texas oil/gas industry, construction, and heavy-duty jobsite work
- "Iron" speaks to steel, durability, and the core material these tools cut through
- Works as a brand name, domain name, and social handle
- Feels authentic and industrial without being generic

### Tagline

"Texas-tough tools for real steel work."

### Member Program Name

**The Yard Crew**

### Brand Hashtag

`#RigIronBuilds`
